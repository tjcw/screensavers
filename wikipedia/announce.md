i= Linux as a Windows screensaver =

https://developer.ibm.com/tutorials/awb-build-linux-dist-as-a-ms-windows-screen-saver/ shows how to build a Windows screensaver that is a Linux distribution. The idea is to customise the Linux distribution with boot splash screen and wallpaper that is your business logo.

https://www.linux.com/news/linux-as-a-screensaver-for-windows-the-gift-of-open-source-games-and-sboms-for-the-holidays/ and https://www.researchgate.net/publication/272094609_Linux_screensaver_for_Windows are previous articles on the subject.

You can download the media kits from here https://archive.org/details/@chris_ward523 or here https://sourceforge.net/projects/linux-screensavers-for-windows/ .

There is a YouTube video here https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J7Jc7uPmLx8 .

Tools to generate screensavers are here https://github.com/tjcw/screensavers . Documentation including revisable documentation is here https://github.com/tjcw/screensaver-docs .
